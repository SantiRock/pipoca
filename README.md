# Pipoca
Pipoca Tunes

Pipoca is a music project that uses artificial intelligence processes as a tool for exploration and creation, not as an end, but as a means. We use AI to explore musical genres, traditions and cultural intersections that interest us. The process starts from open ideas and, from what emerges, we select, curate, edit and intervene in the material until it reaches a place that makes sense to us.

For us, artificial intelligence does not replace musical practice nor seek to replace musicians or producers; it is simply another tool that expands the ways music can be researched, composed and produced.

We are not fixed to this technology: we use these tools today, but that may change in the future. We are open to collaborating with musicians, producers and artists and, if at some point artificial intelligence no longer contributes to what we are looking for, we will simply stop using it
